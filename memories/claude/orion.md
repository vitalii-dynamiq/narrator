# Orion Lyon — 2024 Facility Fire Insurance Recovery

## TL;DR
An insurance settlement from a 2024 Lyon facility fire is finalising around Q1-2026 close. Booked as "other operating income" in the French statutory statements, it inflates the reported Q1 EBITDA for Orion Lyon. Run-rate / LTM-multiple-based valuations should exclude it.

## Context
The 2024 Lyon production facility fire damaged equipment and caused several months of throughput disruption. AXA settlement was negotiated over 18 months; the recovery books below the line as "other operating income" and lands in the Q1-2026 Actual EBITDA figure.

## What to check at close
When the question touches Orion Lyon performance or valuation:
- The reported Q1 Actual EBITDA is inflated by the one-off.
- Adjusted/run-rate EBITDA (for LTM multiple-based valuation) should exclude the settlement.
- Year-over-year comps (Q1-2024 → Q1-2025 → Q1-2026) will look flattering on reported basis; honest picture needs the adjustment.
- If the question is "is Orion's underlying operation improving?" — the answer depends on run-rate EBITDA, not headline.
