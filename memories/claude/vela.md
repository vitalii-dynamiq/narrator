# Vela SE — Nordics Enterprise Cohort Go-Live

## TL;DR
A Swedish enterprise cohort anchored by a large BFSI-led contract went live end of Q4-2025. The investment case rested on operating leverage: fixed platform cost, variable gross margin, incremental enterprise revenue flowing through to EBITDA. Q1-2026 is the first period where the inflection should be visible.

## Status
Open · inflection expected in Q1-2026 actuals.

## Entity refs
ENT_VELA_SE, PRJ_VELA

## Context
The cohort is a handful of enterprise logos across Nordics BFSI. Contracts are multi-year, paid annually in advance, ARR-recognised from go-live in Nov-2025. The thesis assumes high gross margin on incremental revenue and limited new opex through 2026 — so a visible EBITDA-margin lift vs prior quarters is the signal the deal is working.

## What to check at close
When the question touches Vela performance:
- Query EBITDAMarginPct at YTD-2026-03 on Actual vs prior-year YTD (YTD-2025-03) and vs Budget-2026.
- The interesting comparison is the Q1 margin against Q1-2025 — that's the measure of the operating-leverage inflection.
- If the margin hasn't lifted at all, the operating-leverage thesis hasn't yet flowed through. Flag.
- ARR in Actual vs Budget-2026 is a secondary check — did the cohort's revenue land as modelled.
